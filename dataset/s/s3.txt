ATP World Tour Finals 2016: Live coverage on BBC TV and radio
BBC Sport has live coverage of the ATP World Tour Finals from 13-20 November on TV, radio and online.
New world number one Andy Murray will face Stan Wawrinka, Kei Nishikori and Marin Cilic in his group, with world number two Novak Djokovic - winner of this event five times - drawn in the other group.
BBC TV will show eight singles matches, including the first semi-final and the final, while full radio commentary of the tournament is also available.
All BBC TV and radio coverage will be available online and on the BBC Sport app, while the BBC Sport website will also have video clips from the live TV matches (UK only).
